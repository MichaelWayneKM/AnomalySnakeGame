# 🐍 Anomaly Snake

**Don't just play the game. Survive the anomaly.**

Anomaly Snake is a high-octane, ASMR-infused arcade experience built with Kotlin. It takes the classic snake formula and injects it with cinematic boss battles, a synth-heavy soundscape, and a persistent, hungry Anomaly that wants to eat your high score.

[![Anomaly Snake Gameplay Showcase](https://img.youtube.com/vi/c-Bpxp6Jcy0/maxresdefault.jpg)](https://youtu.be/c-Bpxp6Jcy0)

---

### 🕹️ How to Play
* **Navigate:** Use `Arrows` or `WASD` to command your snake.
* **The Hunt:** Eat glowing orbs to score, but avoid the toxic purple ones.
* **The Anomaly:** Once you reach 150 points, the Boss spawns. Out-collect it by grabbing 5 Cyan Soul Orbs to trigger the **Devour Sequence** and claim victory.

---

###  The Tech
* **Engine:** Pure Kotlin/JVM with a custom Java Swing vector-art renderer.
* **Audio:** Bespoke, thread-safe `AudioPlayer` engine for low-latency ASMR soundscapes.
* **Architecture:** Multi-state game loop with a cinematic sequencing engine.
